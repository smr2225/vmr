Article 1. The Second Martian Republic is a constitutional union of citizen associations through councils, aimed at developing citizen freedom, understood as the multiplication of opportunities for harmonious citizen development, based on the expanded reproduction of society as the foundation for humanity's sustainable future as a spacefaring civilization.
Article 2. The citizen is the foundation of the Republic, its primary subject and source of power.
Article 3. The citizen is obliged to uphold the Constitution.
Article 4. The citizen has rights to: life support and social services; as well as personal and political rights, the exercise of which shall not infringe upon the rights and freedoms of others. These are ensured by executive bodies of the Republic through responsible citizens appointed by them.
Article 5. Citizen associations form the basis of the Republic‚Äôs governance structure.
Article 6. Citizen associations are represented in the state structure by their Councils.
Article 7. Councils in their direct work follow the principles of direct citizen self-governance.
Article 8. Councils constitute the supreme representative authority in the Republic. Councils are built starting from primary councils, with votes delegated to delegates in the next-level council, sequentially forming a pyramid of state-territorial councils up to the Supreme Council of the Republic, and for interest-based councils up to their highest councils.
