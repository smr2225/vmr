Basic Parameters

Calendar start: December 1, year 0 SMR. Long years (669 days): 0, 2, 4, 5, 7, 9 in decade. Short years (668 days): 1, 3, 6, 8. Every 108 years another short year is added.

Year Structure

12 months of 56 or 55 days. 8 weeks per month. Short months (55 days): January, May, November. In short years August also becomes short. Each month begins on Monday.

Year 1 (668 days)

Week Structure

7 or 6 days: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday. In short months the last Sunday is skipped.
