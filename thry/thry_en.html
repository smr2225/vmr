Ascension. Matter

Matter Life Mind

The ascent of matter as qualitative leaps in the development of the organization of matter's forms, driven by a decreasing energy level caused by the expansion of the metric.

Philosophers of the scientific school have observed that matter is in motion, and these movements, over the past 14 billion years, have formed a clear picture of material development, which we present here.
